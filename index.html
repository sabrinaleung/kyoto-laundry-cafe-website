Cafe and Coin Laundry in One

A warm and comfortable Brooklyn-style cafe in Saiin.
With a reasonably priced menu, a nice atmosphere, and vegetarian options,
the Kyoto Laundry Cafe is a great place to relax and enjoy good food and drink.

Menu

We have an assortment of delicious and homely dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
There are also vegetarian options and dishes catered toward children.

Chicken Salad Bowl Lunch Set, 1000 yen

The joint establishment of a coin laundry and cafe means that clothes are clean, and the food is clean. With the keyword of "healthy," we have a vegetable lunch (salad bowl) ready for you. This is a fusion bowl inspired by New York salad bowls. Based on the vegetables from our contracted farmers, Kyo-Yasai, give the crispy texture of the Western-style rice bowl a try! The Keema curry uses beans for meat, and food enthusiasts love our Bibimbap salad bowl, all of which are vegetable-based. The KLC original cafe plate with a soup and drink included is available for lunch and dinner!

Keema Curry Salad Bowl, 1000 yen

Our neighboring country of Korea has the wholesome Bibimbap, a dish that is great for our health! There probably aren't many cafes where you can eat Bibimbap. Have this healthy Bibimbap salad bowl that is garnering a lot of attention and become energetic and beautiful. The neighboring kimchi and gochujang, made by Matsubara-san (who is Korean) is extraordinary.

Hamburger Lunch Set, 1000 yen

Double meat patties for lots of volume! Drizzled with Cobb sauce for a lighter flavored hamburger, it's popular with the local foreigners too. With French fries and a drink included, this is perfect for a light brunch. You can also take it to-go.

Omurice Meal Set, 1000 yen

Not the trendy fluffy egg omelets, but an omurice that the cafe has been serving since the past. The sauce is a homemade original demi glace sauce. The simple omurice is popular and fulfills the wishes of the regulars.

Hamburg Steak Lunch Set, 1000 yen

The hamburg steak is hotel restaurant-grade beef (150 grams and above). This was the menu cooked up for the gentlement to come back for. As intended, salarymen order it, and a cafe where you can eat your fill came into creation. We have confidence in the amount of meat!

[ Dinner Only Menu ] Chicken and Vegetable Oven-Bake Galore

Chicken and Kyoto vegetables cooked in a skillet then oven-baked, a menu to go with your beer. For people who are waiting for their laundry to finish and want a drink, or you want to have a nighttime glass of wine at a cafe, this was the menu thought up for you. It's simply seasoned with olive oil and butter, but the herb chicken will surely satisfy your nightly cravings.

Blend Coffee, Flavored Drinks

Because it's a laundry cafe, they won't serve coffee? To prove this wrong, we offer espresso for a pleasant coffee time. We use Brazilian and Sumatra coffee beans from the NY Brooklyn Company. The staff have undergone barista training, and although not the best, please try a satisfying cup of coffee made with our treasured espresso machine, La Marzocco.
